Fun Facts About Sharks

Animal Description

Sharks are a group of fish with skeletons made of cartilage, five to seven gill slits, and fins. They are cold-blooded, meaning they have a body temperature that is similar to the water they live in.

Migration Facts

Shark migration is the large-scale movement of sharks from one area to another. Sharks migrate for a variety of reasons, including food, reproduction, and temperature regulation.
